## Full description (4000 chars max)
DotTime is a clean, minimal home screen widget that visualizes how much of your year, month, day, and hour has passed — using simple dot indicators.

Each row shows 12 dots:
● White dots = time that has passed
● Red dot = where you are now
● Gray dots = time remaining

Four rows track your progress through:
• The current year (e.g. "2026 — 28%")
• The current month (e.g. "APR — 40%")
• The current day (e.g. "SUN — 58%")
• The current hour (e.g. "14h — 33%")

Features:
• Pure black background — perfect for AMOLED screens
• Monospace typography for a clean, technical aesthetic
• No internet, no accounts, no tracking — fully offline
• Automatic updates every 15 minutes
• Lightweight: uses minimal battery and memory
• No app to open — widget only

DotTime is inspired by the idea that visualizing time makes you more intentional about how you spend it. A glance at your home screen tells you exactly where you are in the day, month, and year.

No ads. No in-app purchases. No data collection. Ever.
