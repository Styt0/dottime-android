DotTime — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

DotTime is a home screen widget that displays time progress using dot indicators. It is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

DotTime does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not require any permissions
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not use advertising
  • Does not create user accounts
  • Does not access contacts, location, camera, or any other device data

Third-Party Services

DotTime does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Children's Privacy

DotTime does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
