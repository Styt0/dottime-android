# Privacy Policy — DotTime

**Last updated: April 13, 2026**

## Overview
DotTime ("the App") is a home screen widget that displays time progress. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user data.

## Data Collection
DotTime does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App:

- Does not require an internet connection
- Does not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device sensors
- Does not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not require user accounts or registration

## Data Processing
The App reads the current date and time from your device's system clock to calculate progress percentages. This information is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services
DotTime does not integrate with any third-party services or SDKs that collect data.

## Children's Privacy
DotTime does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.
